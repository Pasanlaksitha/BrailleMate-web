BrailleMate Conveter

How to Use

1 Choose Conversion Type

Select whether you want to convert from Braille to Text or Text to Braille. This determines the direction of conversion.

2 Upload Your Document

Click the "Upload Document" button to select your file.

  • Images (.jpg, .png)
  • Text documents (.txt)
  • Word documents (.doc, .docx)

3 Select Language

Choose the language of your document from the dropdown menu. This ensures accurate conversion.

4 Convert

Accept the terms and conditions, then click "Convert Document" to start the conversion process.
