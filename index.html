MORE ABOUT BRAILLEMATE

Empowering the Visually Impaired

BrailleMate is a cutting-edge accessibility tool designed to bridge the gap between traditional Braille and digital text. By enabling Braille-to-text and text-to-Braille conversion, we empower visually impaired individuals to access education, communication, and information more seamlessly.

  • Real-time Braille-to-Text conversion
  • Text-to-Braille Conversion
  • Dual-Language Support
  • AI-powered accessibility
  • Mobile-friendly, no expensive hardware
  • Designed for Braille & Non Braille users

Braille-to-Text Conversion

Easily convert Braille documents into readable text in seconds, making content accessible for all.

  • Supports Sinhala & English Braille
  • Accurate Optical Recognition for seamless conversion
  • Works on any smartphone – no expensive hardware needed

Text-to-Braille Translation

Convert printed text into Braille-ready format for visually impaired users.

  • Generate Braille documents instantly
  • Compatible with Braille embossers
  • Provides accessibility in education & workplaces
